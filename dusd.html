Dublin Unified School District

Throughout Dublin Unified School District, volunteers from Dublin High School volunteer at 5+ schools in the district, inspiring a curiosity in STEM.

This initiative aims to engage students in interactive and hands-on science experiments, fostering a deep-rooted interest in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).
